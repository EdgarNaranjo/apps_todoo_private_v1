Online Job applicants portal. The candidate(s) and applicant(s) can to apply from the portal and check the status of applications on my account portal of your website.

For this module the user can make the following options:

  • The person applies to a job offer
  • You can access your account on the employee portal and view your applications
  • Review the list of applications
  • See the details of their applications
  • Communicate through the chat any error about the recruitment proccess

The workflow from a recruitment proccess:

The person applies to a job offer



My Applications on Portal or Account Page



My Application Lists



My Application Details Page on My Account



Internal and direct communication from the job offer itself



Future developments:

  • Integration with Reporting Qweb (Download and Print Documents)
  • Improved 'application form' for employment
  • New filters in Portal View
