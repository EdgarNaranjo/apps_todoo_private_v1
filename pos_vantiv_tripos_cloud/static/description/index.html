Allow credit card POS payments with triPOS Cloud. This module allows customers to pay for their orders with credit cards. The transactions are processed by triPOS Cloud.

For this module the user can make the following options:

  • Connecting a Vantiv payment terminal allows you to offer a fluid payment flow.
  • Fluid payment flow to your customers and ease the work of your workers.
  • Add your "vantiv" payment method to your odoo settings.
  • Use "vantiv" at the Point of Sale.

The workflow for add the payment method:

Menu options to create new payment method "vantiv"



Create a new payment method for Vantiv, select the payment terminal option Vantiv, and create new Vantiv credentials.



After creating the "vantiv" credentials, the "lane" must also be configured.



If everything was configured correctly in your PoS interface, at the moment of the payment, you could select your Vantiv payment method.
