Generate invoices automatically if deliveries are validated.

This module automatically generates and posts invoices when deliveries are validated.

  • From a delivery note related to a sales order.
  • By confirming the delivery note, the invoice is automatically generated and published.

The workflow is as follows:


The "Automatic invoice Delivered" option is configured in the "Settings".



After confirming the delivery note, the related invoice will be generated.



The invoice is related to the sales order.



The created invoice is published automatically.
